How to Export as PDF

  1. Click "View" to open the postcard design
  2. Press Cmd + P (Mac) or Ctrl + P (Windows)
  3. Select "Save as PDF" as the destination
  4. Set paper size to "Custom" - 9" x 6"
  5. Set margins to "None"
  6. Enable "Background graphics"
  7. Save the PDF

EDDM Specifications

  • Size: 6" x 9" (standard EDDM postcard)
  • Includes EDDM indicia (postage paid mark)
  • ECRWSS addressing for "Local Postal Customer"
  • Front: Marketing message and visual
  • Back: Details, QR code, and mailing panel
