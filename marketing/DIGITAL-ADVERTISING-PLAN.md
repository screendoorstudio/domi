# DoMi Digital Advertising Plan

## Overview
Digital advertising supports EDDM campaigns with geo-targeted awareness and retargeting. Budget scales from $3,800 (Month 2) to $15,000+ (Month 10+).

---

## Platform Strategy

### Primary Platforms

| Platform | Purpose | Budget % | Best For |
|----------|---------|----------|----------|
| Meta (FB/IG) | Awareness + retargeting | 50% | Homeowners 35-65, environmentally conscious |
| Nextdoor | Hyper-local | 25% | Neighborhood targeting, social proof |
| Google Ads | Intent capture | 25% | People searching "stop junk mail" |

### Secondary Platforms (Phase 3+)
- YouTube: Video ads for broader awareness
- TikTok: Younger homeowners, viral potential
- Programmatic: Scale targeting

---

## Campaign Structure

### Campaign 1: EDDM Support (Geo-Targeted Awareness)

**Objective:** Reach households in EDDM zip codes before/during postcard delivery

**Targeting:**
- Location: Specific zip+4 areas receiving EDDM
- Age: 30-65
- Home ownership: Homeowners (FB targeting)
- Interests: Environmental, organization, home improvement

**Creative Themes:**
1. "Tired of junk mail?" (problem awareness)
2. "Your neighbors are stopping junk mail" (social proof)
3. "Earn $100/month from your mailbox" (earnings angle)

**Budget:** $2,000/month for pilot markets

**Ad Formats:**
- FB/IG: Single image, carousel, video (15-30 sec)
- Nextdoor: Sponsored posts, conversation ads

---

### Campaign 2: Retargeting (Website Visitors)

**Objective:** Convert website visitors who didn't download app

**Targeting:**
- Website visitors (pixel-based)
- Landing page visitors by page
- Time on site > 30 seconds
- Exclude: Already downloaded app

**Creative Themes:**
1. "Still getting junk mail?" (reminder)
2. "Join [X] neighbors who stopped junk mail" (social proof)
3. Testimonial snippets

**Budget:** $500/month initially, scale with traffic

**Sequence:**
- Day 1-3: Soft reminder
- Day 4-7: Value prop reinforcement
- Day 8-14: Urgency/scarcity
- Day 15+: Long-tail nurture

---

### Campaign 3: Lookalike/Interest Expansion

**Objective:** Find new users similar to converters

**Targeting:**
- Lookalike audiences from:
  - App downloaders
  - Verified users
  - High-engagement users
- Interest-based:
  - Environmental/sustainability
  - Organization/minimalism
  - Personal finance
  - Home services

**Budget:** Scale in Phase 2-3 ($2,000-5,000/month)

---

**Objective:** Capture high-intent searches

## Tracking & Attribution

### Pixel/SDK Setup
- [ ] Facebook Pixel on all landing pages
- [ ] Google Analytics 4 with enhanced ecommerce
- [ ] Nextdoor conversion tracking
- [ ] App install attribution (AppsFlyer/Adjust)

### Key Events to Track
| Event | Platform | Description |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| PageView | All | Landing page visit |
| Lead | All | Email capture |
| AppInstall | Mobile | App downloaded |
| Verification | App | Address verified |
| FirstOptOut | App | First mail scanned |

### UTM Convention
```
utm_source: facebook, instagram, nextdoor, google
utm_medium: cpc, social, display
utm_campaign: [campaign_name]-[month]-[year]
utm_content: [creative_variant]
utm_term: [audience_name] or [keyword]
```

### Attribution Model
- 7-day click, 1-day view for awareness
- 28-day click for conversion campaigns
- Last-click for direct response

## Compliance Notes

### Platform Policies
- No misleading claims about income
- Disclaimer for "up to $100/month" earnings claims
- No targeting based on sensitive categories
- Comply with CCPA/privacy requirements

### Ad Copy Disclaimers
```
*Earnings vary based on offer engagement. Average user earns $30-50/month.
```

### Landing Page Requirements
- Clear privacy policy link
- Terms of service link
- "How we make money" transparency
- Unsubscribe option for any email capture
