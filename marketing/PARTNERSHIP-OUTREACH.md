### Partnership Tiers

| Tier | Commitment | DoMi Provides | CU Provides |
|------|------------|---------------|-------------|
| **Awareness** | None | Member education materials | Newsletter/email mention |
| **Preferred** | $0 | Co-branded landing page, priority support | Dedicated promotion, logo use |
| **Integrated** | Revenue share | API integration, white-label option | Deep integration, exclusive territory |

### Objection Handling

**"How do you verify addresses?"**
> We use a combination of document verification (ID + mail piece photo) and integration with USPS Informed Delivery. This creates a verified Domi ID tied to a real, confirmed address.

**"How is member data protected?"**
> We're SOC 2 compliant [or pursuing], data is encrypted at rest and in transit, and members always control what's shared. Happy to provide our security documentation.

**"What's the business model?"**
> We're a marketplace connecting verified consumers with brands who want to reach them. Revenue comes from brands, not consumers. Credit unions can participate in referral revenue.

### Stage Definitions
1. **Prospect** - Identified, not yet contacted
2. **Contacted** - Initial outreach sent
3. **Engaged** - Response received, conversation started
4. **Discovery** - Meeting scheduled or held
5. **Proposal** - Partnership terms shared
6. **Negotiation** - Terms being discussed
7. **Closed Won** - Partnership signed
8. **Closed Lost** - Declined

### Follow-Up Cadence
- Day 0: Initial email
- Day 3: Follow-up email
- Day 7: LinkedIn connection request
- Day 10: Phone call attempt
- Day 14: Final email (if no response)
- Day 30: Re-engage with new angle
