## Email Design Guidelines

### Brand Voice
- Friendly, not corporate
- Empowering, not preachy
- Specific, not vague
- Action-oriented

### Technical Specs
- From name: Domi or Steve Gianfilippo
- From email: hello@digitaldomi.com
- Reply-to: support@digitaldomi.com
- Preheader: 40-60 characters
- Body: 150-250 words max
- CTA: Single primary action per email

### A/B Tests to Run
| Element | Test A | Test B |
|---------|--------|--------|
| Subject | Question | Statement |
| Sender | "Domi" | "Steve from Domi" |
| CTA | "Open App" | "Stop Junk Mail" |
| Tone | Stats-focused | Story-focused |

### Frequency Rules
- Max 1 email per day
- Max 3 emails per week
- Suppress during re-engagement if user becomes active
- Always respect unsubscribes immediately
