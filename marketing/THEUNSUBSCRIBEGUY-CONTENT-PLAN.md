**Domain:** TheUnsubscribeGuy.com
**Purpose:** SEO/AEO content hub driving organic traffic to DOMI

## AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) Strategy

### Voice Search Targets
Optimize for conversational queries that appear in voice assistants and AI responses.

**Featured Snippet Targets:**
- "How do I stop junk mail" - List format
- "Why do I get so much junk mail" - Paragraph format
- "Is it illegal to throw away junk mail" - Direct answer
- "How many trees are cut down for junk mail" - Statistic format

**Content Format for AEO:**
```markdown
## [Question as H2]
[Direct answer in first 40-60 words]
[Supporting detail]
[Numbered list if applicable]
```
