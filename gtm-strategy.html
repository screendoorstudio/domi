EDDM Postcard Campaigns

Our anchor channel: Three-touch postcard sequences reaching every mailbox in target neighborhoods

Touch 1: "Yes, This is Junk Mail"

Ironic hook that cuts through - the last piece of junk mail they'll ever need.

  • Red gradient design, bold headline
  • QR to opt-out landing page
  • Problem-focused messaging

Touch 2: "Coupons You Want. Zero Clutter."

Digital mailbox value prop with earnings mentioned. 2-3 weeks after Touch 1.

  • Blue gradient, convenience focus
  • "$100/mo" earnings callout
  • 3-step how-it-works visual

Touch 3: "$100 Per Month"

Heavy earnings focus with DoMi-branded "Return to Sender" sticker pack included.

  • Lime green gradient, money focus
  • DoMi "Return to Sender" sticker pack
  • Earnings breakdown + testimonial

Neighborhood Selection

Data-driven targeting for maximum conversion potential.

  • HH income: $75K-$150K
  • High environmental consciousness
  • Strong Nextdoor activity
  • Low renter ratio

Digital Advertising

Geo-targeted campaigns supporting EDDM with awareness, retargeting, and intent capture

Meta Advertising (FB + Instagram)

Primary paid channel at 50% of digital budget. Geo-targeted to EDDM zip codes.

  • Awareness: "Tired of junk mail?" problem hook
  • Social Proof: "Your neighbors are stopping junk mail"
  • Earnings: "Earn $100/month from your mailbox"
  • Retargeting: 7-day view, 28-day click windows
  • Lookalikes: From verified users + engagers

Nextdoor Advertising

Hyper-local platform perfect for neighborhood-by-neighborhood expansion.

  • Sponsored Posts: Native-looking recommendations
  • Conversation Ads: "Anyone else drowning in junk mail?"
  • Survey Tool: Poll neighborhoods on EDDM preferences
  • Target 25% of digital budget

Google Search Ads

Capture high-intent searchers actively looking for junk mail solutions.

  • "stop junk mail" - $1.50 CPC
  • "opt out junk mail" - $1.30 CPC
  • "junk mail app" - $0.80 CPC
  • 25% of digital budget

Video Creative
